rScout 2018 app
The rScout system is an android app used for the FIRST Robotics Competition. In these competitions, high school teams build robots to compete in a sports like game. The competition is divided into two parts, qualifications and eliminations. Teams performance in the qualification rounds determine their placement for eliminations, and the top 8 teams particiapte in a draft of the remaining teams to determine alliances for the elimination bracket. This app allows the collection of data to make more informed decision during the draft portion of the competition, as well as be more prepared for individual opponents. The system operates by taking data through custom UI elements which mimic the playing field, packaging data about an individual match, and storing the data on a standalone server hosted on a Raspberry Pi. The entire system works completely offline and independently, with internet capabilities to pull event information and work wirelessly when available.
